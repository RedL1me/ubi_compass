# Ecosystem Orchestrator

An ecosystem orchestrator is an individual or organization that strategically manages and coordinates the interactions and relationships among various participants within an innovation ecosystem. They create networks where startups, corporations, investors, academia, and other stakeholders collaborate effectively, fostering value creation and mutual growth through coordinated efforts and shared resources.
## Did You Know
Ecosystem orchestrators facilitate connections, align strategic objectives, and create frameworks for collaboration among diverse participants. They provide leadership and governance structures that enable effective coordination while maintaining flexibility for innovation. Through platforms, events, and strategic initiatives, orchestrators ensure that ecosystem participants can leverage each other's strengths to accelerate growth and drive collective success.
## Bonus Fact
This role is increasingly critical as innovation becomes more collaborative and interdependent across industries and geographies.

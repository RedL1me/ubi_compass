# Ecosystem Orchestration Framework

The Ecosystem Orchestration Framework refers to the systematic approach used by an orchestrator to manage, coordinate, and align diverse participants, resources, and activities within a business ecosystem. This framework facilitates seamless collaboration, innovation, and value co-creation among stakeholders such as suppliers, partners, customers, and regulators, enabling the ecosystem to achieve shared goals efficiently.
### Did You Know
The framework covers key functions including stakeholder management, resource allocation, governance, communication, and performance measurement. It supports dynamic adaptation to market changes and technological shifts by fostering agile interactions and co-development, enabling stakeholders to leverage collective strengths.
### Bonus Fact
Frameworks such as the "5E Model" (Enrich, Engage, Execute, Evolve, Expand) guide organizations in maximizing partner performance and revenue generation within ecosystems. As ecosystems grow more complex, orchestration frameworks will be increasingly vital for sustaining innovation, competitiveness, and resilience.

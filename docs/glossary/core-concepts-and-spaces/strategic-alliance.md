# Strategic Alliance

A strategic alliance is a formal agreement between two or more independent organizations to cooperate in achieving shared strategic objectives. It allows partners to combine resources, expertise, and capabilities to create value that neither could achieve alone while remaining separate entities.
## Did You Know
Strategic alliances enable partners to enhance innovation, access new markets, reduce risks, and improve competitive positioning. Many strategic alliances have been formed through UBI Global membership and events. Click Join UBI Global on the top right of this screen to find out more about membership.
## Bonus Fact
Strategic alliances offer flexibility compared to mergers and acquisitions by enabling quicker, less regulated collaborations that preserve organizational independence.

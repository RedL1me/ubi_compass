# Innovation Ecosystem

An innovation ecosystem is a dynamic, interconnected network of organizations, individuals, resources, and institutions that collaborate to generate, develop, and scale innovative ideas, products, or services. It includes startup incubators and accelerators, the startups themselves, along with corporations, universities, investors, government bodies, research institutions, and other stakeholders working together to foster creativity and economic growth.
## Did You Know
Innovation ecosystems thrive on active collaboration, knowledge exchange, and co-creation among peers as well as diverse actors. These ecosystems facilitate access to funding, expertise, infrastructure, and markets, enabling innovators to experiment, accelerate development, and bring new solutions to market.
## Bonus Fact
Incubators and accelerators play pivotal roles by connecting startups with partners, mentorship, and capital within these ecosystems.

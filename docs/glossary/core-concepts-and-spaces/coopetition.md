# Coopetition

Coopetition is a business strategy where companies simultaneously cooperate and compete. It involves partnering with competitors or related firms to achieve common goals—such as innovation, market expansion, or resource sharing—while continuing to compete in other business areas.
### Did You Know
Organizations engaging in coopetition collaborate on joint projects like research and development, setting industry standards, or pooling resources for efficiency gains. At the same time, they remain rivals in areas like pricing or market share. This dual dynamic requires clear agreements, trust, and strategic clarity to balance competitive and cooperative interests effectively.
### Bonus Fact
Coopetition fosters market growth, accelerates innovation, and optimizes resources by shifting from zero-sum to positive-sum business dynamics. Properly managed coopetition supports stronger ecosystems, innovation communities, and sustainable competitive advantage.

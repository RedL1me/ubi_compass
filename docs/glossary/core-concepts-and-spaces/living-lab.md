# Living Lab

A Living Lab is a real-world, user-centric innovation environment where diverse stakeholders — including citizens, businesses, academic institutions, and government bodies — collaborate to co-create, prototype, test, and validate new solutions. These innovation ecosystems prioritize active involvement of end-users, enabling development of practical and sustainable innovations directly in authentic settings.
### Did you know...
Living Labs facilitate dynamic, iterative innovation processes by integrating real-life testing with systematic user feedback. This engagement model enables joint value creation among participants as they exchange insights, experiment with new ideas, and adapt solutions responsively to actual needs. By breaking down traditional silos between users, researchers, and industry, Living Labs accelerate the design and adoption of impactful products, services, and policies.

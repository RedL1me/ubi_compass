# Impact Innovation

Impact innovation refers to approaches that balance commercial objectives with broader social, environmental, or community benefits. It emphasizes developing new products, services, or business models that generate positive, lasting change alongside financial returns, making a meaningful contribution to society.
## Did You Know
Startups and organizations practicing impact innovation integrate mission-driven goals with market-driven strategies. They collaborate with diverse stakeholders to address pressing societal challenges while sustaining economic viability. Impact innovation often incorporates sustainability, social equity, and ethical considerations into the innovation process, fostering solutions aligned with global needs.
## Bonus Fact
Impact innovation is increasingly vital in addressing global challenges such as climate change, inequality, and health disparities. Companies that integrate impact principles often gain a competitive advantage by appealing to conscious consumers and investors. Measuring and communicating impact strengthens stakeholder trust and supports scaling transformative solutions.

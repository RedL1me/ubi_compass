# Ecosystem Partnership

An ecosystem partnership is a network of businesses, organizations, and stakeholders working together to create mutual value and address evolving market needs. This collaborative approach leverages the unique strengths, resources, and expertise of each partner to foster innovation, expand market reach, and enhance customer solutions within the broader innovation ecosystem.
## Did You Know
Partners collaborate on co-innovation, co-investment, joint marketing, and resource sharing, often across industries and geographies. These partnerships enable participants to access new customers, technologies, and markets more effectively than isolated efforts.

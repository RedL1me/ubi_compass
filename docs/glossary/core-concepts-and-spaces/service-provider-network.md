# Service Provider Network

A service provider network is a coordinated group of professional organizations and specialists that supply vital support services—such as legal, financial, marketing, HR, and technical expertise—to startups, scaleups, and innovation ecosystem participants. These networks connect founders and ecosystem actors with trusted partners needed to build, grow, and sustain their businesses.
## Did You Know
Service provider networks operate through strategic partnerships, directories, or curated platforms linked to incubators, accelerators, or innovation hubs. They streamline access to specialized resources and solutions, often negotiating preferential rates or dedicated support for ecosystem members. This interconnected support infrastructure enhances the value proposition of startup programs, enabling founders to focus on growth and innovation.

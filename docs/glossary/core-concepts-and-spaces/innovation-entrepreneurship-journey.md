# Innovation Entrepreneurship Journey

The Innovation Entrepreneurship Journey represents the stages an entrepreneur or startup moves through to transform a novel idea into a successful, scalable business. It reflects the continuous evolution from ideation and validation to development, market entry, growth, and sustained impact within the innovation ecosystem.
### Did You Know
Entrepreneurs navigate distinct but interconnected phases, including idea generation, opportunity evaluation, product development, pilot testing, commercialization, and scaling. Each stage demands specific skills, resources, and strategies. Support organizations like incubators, accelerators, and ecosystem enablers provide critical guidance and resources tailored to these phases to increase the likelihood of success.

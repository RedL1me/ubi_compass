# Unicorn Startup

A unicorn startup is a privately held company valued at $1 billion or more. The term “decacorn” refers to startups valued at $10 billion or more. These valuations typically arise from funding rounds led by venture capital, private equity, or significant corporate investments, based on investors’ long-term growth expectations rather than current profits.
## Did You Know
Unicorns are often characterized by disruptive business models, rapid scaling, and strong market influence. They operate primarily in technology-driven sectors and demonstrate hyper-growth trajectories, attracting substantial venture capital funding.
## Bonus Fact
There were 1,450 privately held unicorn startups worldwide in 2024. The United States led with over half of the world’s unicorns, followed by China and India as the second and third-largest hosts of unicorn startups.

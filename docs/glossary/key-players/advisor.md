# Advisor

An advisor is an experienced professional who provides specialized knowledge, guidance, and support to startups across various areas such as finance, marketing, legal, or technology. Advisors assist founders in making informed decisions, solving complex challenges, and optimizing business strategies, often in a flexible and project-specific capacity.
## Did You Know
Advisors typically engage on an as-needed basis, offering targeted expertise rather than ongoing day-to-day coaching or mentorship. They may participate in board meetings, provide strategic consultations, or support fundraising efforts. Startups benefit from advisors’ industry insights, networks, and credibility, which complement internal skills and help navigate growth hurdles.
## Helpful Information
Advisors can be invaluable in filling knowledge gaps, accelerating learning curves, and opening doors to resources. Clear expectations and defined roles help maximize the impact of advisor relationships. Many incubators and accelerators assist startups in identifying and engaging trusted advisors as part of their support services.
## Advisor, Coach or Mentor?
Mentors develop long-term, holistic relationships focused on growth; coaches facilitate structured, performance-driven improvement; and advisors offer specialized, issue-focused guidance. Many startups benefit from engaging all three types at different stages and needs of the venture.

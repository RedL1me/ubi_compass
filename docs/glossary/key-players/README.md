# 4 Key Players

Advisor Trusted Experts Enhancing Startup Decision-Making
Alumni Startup Graduates of Incubator and Accelerator Programs Scaling New Heights
Angel Investor or Angel Group Early-Stage Seed Capital Providers Supporting Startup Growth
Coach Structured Support to Unlock Startup Potential
Ecosystem Builder Catalysts Connecting and Developing Innovation Networks
Ecosystem Enabler Facilitators Strengthening Innovation Networks
Ecosystem Orchestrator Strategic Coordinators of Innovation Networks
Entrepreneur Visionary Individuals Creating and Growing New Ventures
Intrapreneur Driving Innovation Within Established Organizations
Mentor Experienced Guides Supporting Long-Term Entrepreneurial Growth
Serial Entrepreneur Pioneering Multiple Ventures with Experience and Networks
Service Provider Network Connecting Experts and Resources Across the Innovation Ecosystem
Strategic Alliance Collaborative Partnerships Driving Mutual Growth and Competitive Advantage

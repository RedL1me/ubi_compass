# Digital Twin

Virtual representations of products, processes, or systems used for simulation and real-time monitoring. Innovators and incubators utilize digital twins for prototyping, manufacturing optimization, and predictive analytics.
### Did you know...
Digital twins support iterative development by providing a risk-free environment to test scenarios and operational changes before implementing them in the real world. They facilitate deep insight into product behavior, supply chain dynamics, and customer interactions by integrating advanced analytics and AI capabilities. Through these virtual replicas, businesses improve decision-making, minimize maintenance costs, and accelerate innovation cycles across industries.
### Bonus Fact
Digital twins enable companies to anticipate failures, optimize resource use, and personalize customer experiences at scale.

# Quantum Computing Readiness

Preparing startups for emerging quantum technologies, which will drive future breakthroughs in computing, security, and analytics—an area that forward-thinking innovation programs are tracking closely.
### Did you know...
Achieving quantum readiness includes building organizational quantum literacy, establishing governance frameworks, investing in pilot projects, and cultivating partnerships with academia and technology providers. It requires a realistic roadmap balancing early quantum exploration with integration into existing processes and security practices.
### Bonus Fact
Startups benefit from incubator support that helps translate quantum innovations into actionable business opportunities while managing risks related to quantum-safe cryptography and evolving standards.

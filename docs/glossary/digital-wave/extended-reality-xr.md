# Extended Reality (XR)

Umbrella term encompassing virtual, augmented, and mixed reality technologies. XR is increasingly important for startups in education, healthcare, retail, and entertainment, and many incubators now offer XR development support.
## Engagement
XR technologies engage users through immersive experiences enabled by hardware such as VR headsets, AR glasses, and smart devices. Startups leverage XR for product design, remote collaboration, training, and innovative customer experiences. Incubators provide development support to accelerate XR innovation, helping founders build applications that integrate seamlessly into everyday life and industry workflows.

# Business Support Organization (BSO)

A Business Support Organization (BSO) is an entity dedicated to providing specialized assistance and resources to organizations that foster innovation and entrepreneurship, such as startup incubators and accelerators. These support networks include chambers of commerce, university-based innovation centers, industry associations, and other ecosystem partners focused on enabling incubators and accelerators to deliver greater value and achieve excellence within the entrepreneurial landscape.
Did you know...
BSOs play a key role in nurturing high-performing incubators and accelerators by curating knowledge, facilitating networking opportunities, and offering critical services such as benchmarking, educational programs, and research-based insights.

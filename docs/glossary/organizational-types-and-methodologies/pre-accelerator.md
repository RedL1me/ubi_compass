# Pre-Accelerator

A pre-accelerator is an organization or program offering a short, intensive curriculum designed to provide foundational entrepreneurship training to very early-stage founders. It prepares startups for subsequent participation in startup incubators or accelerators by helping founders refine their ideas, identify target customers, and build essential business skills.
### Did You Know
Pre-accelerators typically last a few weeks and focus on mentor-led workshops, customer discovery, market research, and pitch development. Participants gain access to supportive peer communities, industry experts, and practical resources without usually giving up equity. These programs help entrepreneurs clarify value propositions, validate business models, and increase readiness for growth-focused accelerators.
### Bonus Fact
Many founders find that completing a pre-accelerator increases their chances of acceptance into competitive accelerator programs and improves investor readiness.

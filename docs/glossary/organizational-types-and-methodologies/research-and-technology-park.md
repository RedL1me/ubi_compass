# Research and Technology Park

A Research and Technology Park is a property-based facility, usually affiliated with a university, research institution, or government body, designed to foster innovation and economic growth by providing affordable space, infrastructure, and integration opportunities for knowledge-based companies. Unlike incubators or accelerators, these parks typically do not offer structured incubation programming but focus on creating a collaborative environment where tenants benefit from proximity to leading research and development activities.
## Did you know...
These parks serve as hubs for technology transfer, collaboration, and commercialization by hosting startups, established companies, research labs, and sometimes support services. Tenants gain access to specialized facilities, networking opportunities, and partnerships with academic and research entities, facilitating the advancement of technology-driven projects. Management of these parks often includes oversight of operations, community-building efforts, and alignment with the goals of affiliated research organizations.

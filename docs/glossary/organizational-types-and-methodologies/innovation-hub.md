# Innovation Hub

An innovation hub is a physical or virtual space—often strategically located within research parks, urban districts, or dedicated digital platforms—where startups, established companies, academic institutions, and investors gather to exchange ideas, access industry insights, and collaborate on new technological solutions. These hubs are designed to encourage creativity and interaction, nurturing breakthrough innovations within a focused sector or community.
### Did you know...
Innovation hubs facilitate networking, support services, and knowledge-sharing activities through events, workshops, mentorship, and collaborative projects. By assembling talent and resources from various backgrounds, these spaces provide startups and innovators with mentorship, access to funding, state-of-the-art facilities, and a vibrant peer network. Participation in an innovation hub can accelerate product development, foster strategic partnerships, and enable ventures to scale by connecting them to essential markets and insights.

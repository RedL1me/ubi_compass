# Digital Sandbox

A secure, controlled digital environment where startups can experiment with, test, and validate new digital products or technologies before full-scale deployment. Digital sandboxes reduce risk and accelerate innovation cycles.
### Did you know...
Digital sandboxes enable innovators to test proof-of-concepts, refine product features, and evaluate performance in a realistic yet safe setting. They support collaboration across teams and with regulatory bodies, fostering co-creation and feedback loops that drive product improvement. This approach helps startups de-risk development, comply with regulations, and shorten time-to-market while ensuring robustness and scalability.
### Bonus Fact
Originally popularized in financial technology, digital sandboxes have expanded across sectors, including AI, telecommunications, and sustainability.

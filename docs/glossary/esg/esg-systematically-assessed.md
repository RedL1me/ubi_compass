# ESG Systematically Assessed

## Metric Definition
Existence of formal processes for systematic ESG performance assessment within the program.
### Definition
Assess if organizations regularly evaluate ESG criteria.
### Purpose
To move beyond rhetoric and track ESG progress meaningfully, organization-wide.
### Rationale
Systematic assessment enables informed adaptations and policy improvements.
### Advantages and Limitations
Demonstrates accountability and transparency.
Requires dedicated resources and expertise.
Supports continuous ESG performance improvement.
Reporting alone is insufficient without responsive action.

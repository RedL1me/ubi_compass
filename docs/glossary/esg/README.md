# 7 ESG

- ESG — Climate Strategy Formal environmental and climate strategy within the organization
- Community Impact Assessment of social initiatives’ outputs and outcomes
- DEI Monitoring Continuous tracking of diversity, equity, and inclusion metrics
- DEI Strategy Assessment of a formal DEI strategy
- ESG Strategy Verify the existence of an actionable ESG strategy
- ESG Systematically Assessed Assessment of formal process for ESG performance
- ESG Training Assessment of ESG training within the organization
- ESG in Admissions ESG criteria considered in startup admission
- ESG in Funding Measures program commitment to funding ESG-focused startups
- ESG in Goals The integration of ESG principles into organizational mission
- Environmental Impact Monitoring Evaluates programs’ capability to track environmental footprints
- External Auditing Transparent, third-party verification of ESG practices
- Ongoing ESG Requirements Verification of commitment to ESG program via ongoing requirements
- Stakeholder Engagement Formal processes for stakeholder involvement in program governance
- ESG Network Affiliation Confirmation of ESG network affiliation
- ESG Framework Adoption Validation of a framework or set of ESG standards
- DEI Stats Collection and monitoring of DEI statistics
- Environmental Impact Stats Tracking and reporting of environmental stats

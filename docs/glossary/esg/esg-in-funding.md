# ESG in Funding

## Metric Definition
Incorporation of ESG criteria in funding decisions for client startups.
### Definition
Measures program commitment to funding startups that meet ESG or impact standards.
### Purpose
To promote capital flow towards startups with positive ESG outcomes.
### Rationale
Funding aligned with ESG criteria enhances long-term societal benefits.
### Advantages and Limitations
Advantages
Limitations
Supports impact-driven entrepreneurship.
Range of engagement from minimal to exclusive funding of ESG startups.
Reinforces accountability in investment strategies.
ESG definitions can vary widely.

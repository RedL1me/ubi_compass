# DEI Monitoring

## Metric Definition
Continuous tracking of diversity, equity, and inclusion metrics within client startups.
### Definition
Assesses sustained program commitment to equitable and diverse ecosystem development.
### Purpose
To identify trends and progress in social inclusion over time.
### Rationale
Ongoing monitoring is necessary for meaningful DEI advancement.
Supports targeted support and policy refinement.
Scope limited by regulatory and cultural constraints.
Addresses systemic social inequities.
Requires robust data collection and privacy safeguards.

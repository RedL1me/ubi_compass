# ESG in Admissions

## Metric Definition
Consideration of ESG criteria in startup admissions decisions.
### Definition
Assesses whether ESG factors influence which startups are accepted into the program.
### Purpose
To embed ESG values at the earliest point of startup support and selection.
### Rationale
Integrating ESG into admissions aligns the startup portfolio with impact goals.
Advantages
- Encourages responsible entrepreneurship.
- Aligns program strategy with global sustainability trends.
Limitations
- ESG consideration may be cursory or inconsistent.
- Allows wide interpretation and flexibility in application.

# DEI Stats

## Metric Definition: Environmental Impact Stats
Collection and monitoring of diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) statistics among founders.
Definition
Measures program commitment to social ESG aspects by tracking representation and inclusiveness within the startup community.
Purpose
To identify gaps and track progress on equity and inclusion as a foundation for improvement.
Rationale
DEI data collection is essential for addressing disparities and fostering inclusive ecosystems.
Definition:
Supports targeted interventions to reduce social inequities.
Legal and social acceptability varies by country.
Provides a baseline for social impact measurement.
Controversial in some jurisdictions due to concerns about reinforcing divisions.

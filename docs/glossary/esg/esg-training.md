# ESG Training

## Metric Definition
Provision of ESG-related training or workshops to client startups.
Definition
Assesses programs’ efforts to build ESG competence and awareness among startups.
Purpose
To embed ESG knowledge and practices in startup development.
Rationale
Training is key to operationalizing ESG beyond policy statements.
Builds founder capacity for responsible business.
Depth and frequency of training are often unspecified.
Signals program commitment to continuous ESG learning.
ESG aspects covered may vary significantly.

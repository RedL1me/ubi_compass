# ESG Strategy

## Metric Definition
Existence of an actionable ESG strategy operational within the program.
Definition
Measures whether ESG goals are embedded into daily program functions and long-term planning.
Purpose
To ensure ESG integration translates into practical implementation.
Rationale
Strategy operationalization is critical for real ESG impact.
Drives consistency and focus.
May lack follow-through if not supported by leadership and resources.
Facilitates measurable ESG outcomes.
Needs alignment with other organizational functions.

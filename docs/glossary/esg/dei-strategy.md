# DEI Strategy

## Metric Definition
Existence and implementation of a formal diversity, equity, and inclusion strategy within the program.
Definition
Measures organizational-level focus on social equity and inclusive practices.
Purpose
To institutionalize DEI efforts as a core program value.
Rationale
Structured DEI strategies lead to systemic social change.
Enhances program culture and inclusivity.
Overlaps with general ESG strategy.
Supports equitable access and outcomes.
Effectiveness depends on genuine commitment and enforcement.

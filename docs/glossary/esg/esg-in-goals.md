# ESG in Goals

## Metric Definition
Integration of ESG principles into the organization's mission and strategic objectives.
### Definition
Measures whether ESG is embedded at the philosophical and operational core of the program.
### Purpose
To align all program activities with sustainability and social responsibility goals.
### Rationale
Philosophical commitment drives authentic and impactful ESG practice.
Sets foundational tone for organizational behavior.
It may be aspirational without concrete action.
Guides decision-making and resource allocation.
Susceptible to “ESG-washing” risks.

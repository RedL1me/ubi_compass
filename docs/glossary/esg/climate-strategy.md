# Climate Strategy

## Metric Definition
Existence and operation of a formal environmental and climate strategy within the organization.
### Definition
Measures the organization's focus on environmental goals integration at the organizational level.
### Purpose
To embed climate action and sustainability in program operations.
### Rationale
Climate strategies are vital in addressing global and local environmental challenges.
Demonstrates leadership in environmental stewardship.
Potential overlap with broader ESG or environmental impact initiatives.
Aligns program with international sustainability frameworks.
Variable detail and scope across organizations.

# Ongoing ESG Requirements

## Metric Definition
Presence of ongoing ESG compliance requirements for client startups.
### Definition
Measures program enforcement of ESG-related obligations post-admission.
### Purpose
To ensure sustained ESG adherence and accountability among supported startups.
### Rationale
Ongoing requirements encourage continuous improvement and governance.
### Advantages and Limitations
Reinforces ESG integration into business operations.
Monitoring adherence may be resource-intensive.
Supports systemic ESG impact.
Effectiveness depends on enforcement rigor.

# External Auditing

## Metric Definition
Engagement of the organization in independent external ESG or governance audits.
### Definition
Measures transparency and third-party verification of ESG and governance practices.
### Purpose
To enhance credibility and stakeholder trust through objective assessments.
### Rationale
External audits are recognized as best practices in governance.
### Advantages and Limitations
Validates program claims and performance.
Resource intensive, not uniformly feasible.
Encourages continual improvement.
Smaller or private programs are less likely to undergo audits.

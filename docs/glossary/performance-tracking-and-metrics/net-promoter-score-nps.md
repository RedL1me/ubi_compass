# Net Promoter Score (NPS)

Net Promoter Score (NPS) is a customer satisfaction and loyalty metric that measures the likelihood of customers recommending a business, product, or service to others. It is based on a simple survey question asking customers to rate, on a scale of 0 to 10, how likely they are to recommend the company.
## Did You Know
Customers’ responses categorize them into three groups:
- Promoters (score 9-10): Loyal enthusiasts likely to promote the business.
- Passives (score 7-8): Satisfied but not enthusiastic customers.
- Detractors (score 0-6): Unhappy customers who may discourage others.
NPS is calculated by subtracting the percentage of detractors from promoters, resulting in a score between -100 and +100. It provides a clear snapshot of customer sentiment, helping businesses identify strengths and areas for improvement.
## Bonus Fact
NPS is widely regarded as a key indicator of business growth potential, as high NPS scores correlate with stronger customer retention and word-of-mouth referrals. Many leading companies use NPS to benchmark performance within industries and guide customer experience improvements.

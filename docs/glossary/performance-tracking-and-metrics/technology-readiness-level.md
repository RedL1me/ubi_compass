# Technology Readiness Level

Technology Readiness Level (TRL) is a standardized scale used to assess the maturity of a specific technology, ranging from initial conceptualization to full commercial deployment. The scale typically consists of nine levels, where TRL 1 represents basic principles observed and TRL 9 indicates technology proven successful in an operational environment.
## Did You Know
TRL provides a common language for innovators, investors, and policymakers to evaluate progress, identify risks, and allocate resources effectively. It guides decision-making throughout research, development, pilot testing, and commercialization stages. Organizations use TRL assessments to communicate project status, qualify for funding, and plan technology deployment strategies.
## Bonus Fact
Developed by NASA and now widely adopted across industries and governments, TRL scales facilitate transparency and consistency in innovation management. Recognizing that technology development is not always linear, TRL supports flexible assessment tailored to different sectors.

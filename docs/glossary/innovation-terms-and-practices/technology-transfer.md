# Technology Transfer

Technology transfer is the process through which new inventions, scientific discoveries, and innovations created in research institutions, universities, or labs are moved and transformed into commercial products, services, or technologies. This involves licensing intellectual property (IP), creating startups, and partnering with industry to bring innovations to market.
## Did You Know
Technology transfer professionals work closely with researchers, entrepreneurs, legal teams, and industry partners to protect IP, assess commercial potential, secure funding, negotiate agreements, and support startup development. This collaborative process helps accelerate commercialization and economic growth by bridging the gap between early-stage ideas and practical market-ready solutions.
## Helpful Information
Technology transfer is fundamental to innovation ecosystems, enabling research breakthroughs to benefit society and contribute to economic development. Successful transfer requires navigating legal, financial, and operational challenges while fostering partnerships that amplify impact and scalability.

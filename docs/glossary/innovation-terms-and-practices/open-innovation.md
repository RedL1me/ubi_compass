# Open Innovation

Open innovation is a collaborative approach that encourages organizations to source ideas, technologies, and expertise not only from internal teams but also from external partners such as startups, customers, research institutions, and even competitors. This inclusive innovation model accelerates development by leveraging a wider knowledge ecosystem and breaking down traditional R&D silos.
### Did You Know
Open innovation involves actively seeking, integrating, and sharing knowledge beyond organizational boundaries. Companies participate in partnerships, crowdsourcing challenges, co-creation projects, and technology scouting to access diverse perspectives and accelerate product or service development.
### Bonus Fact
Incubators foster open innovation by connecting startups with corporate partners, facilitating knowledge exchange, and supporting joint initiatives.

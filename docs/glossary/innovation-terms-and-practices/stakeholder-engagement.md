# Stakeholder Engagement

## Metric Definition
Existence of formal processes for stakeholder involvement in program governance.
### Definition
Measures program openness to external ecosystem actors influencing policies and accountability.
### Purpose
To ensure transparency and inclusivity in decision-making aligned with ESG principles.
### Rationale
Engaged stakeholders can reduce harmful practices and improve ecosystem trust.
Promotes accountability and diverse perspectives.
Engagement does not guarantee positive ecosystem outcomes.
Enhances trust and program legitimacy.
Resource constraints may limit meaningful involvement.

# Ideation

The creative process of generating, developing, and communicating new ideas for products, services, or business models. It encompasses brainstorming, conceptualizing, and refining ideas aimed at solving problems or capturing new opportunities, serving as the foundation for innovation and growth.
### Did You Know
Ideation typically involves collaborative sessions such as brainstorming, mind mapping, and design thinking workshops that encourage diverse perspectives and open creativity. Startups and incubators utilize structured frameworks to capture, evaluate, and prioritize ideas systematically. Feedback loops and iterative refinement ensure ideas evolve into viable, market-ready solutions.
### Bonus Fact
Incubators play a crucial role by facilitating ideation workshops, providing tools, and helping startups move from idea generation to implementation.

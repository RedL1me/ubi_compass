# Growth Stage

The growth stage of a startup is the phase where the company has achieved product-market fit and is focused on scaling operations, expanding the customer base, and increasing revenue. This stage often involves securing larger investment rounds, hiring key personnel, and refining processes to support rapid and sustainable growth.
### Did You Know
Startups in the growth stage concentrate on optimizing marketing, sales, and customer service to accelerate market penetration. They develop scalable operational frameworks and often enter new geographic or demographic markets. Support from investors, accelerators, and ecosystem partners becomes crucial to manage the increasing complexity and drive continued expansion.
### Bonus Fact
Ecosystem enablers play a key role in providing the capital, mentorship, and partnerships necessary during this phase.

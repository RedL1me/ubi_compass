# Pivot

A pivot is a significant and deliberate shift in a startup's strategy to test a new approach related to the business model, target market, product offering, or revenue model. This change is based on data-driven learning and feedback, aiming to realign the company’s direction to better meet customer needs and market demands.
## Did You Know
Pivoting involves analyzing market signals, customer feedback, and performance metrics to identify when the current strategy is not yielding desired results. Startups execute pivots by adjusting core elements—such as focusing on a specific feature (zoom-in pivot), changing customer segments, or reworking pricing models—to improve product-market fit.
### Bonus Fact
Incubators support this process by helping founders assess data, design pivot strategies, and manage change effectively.

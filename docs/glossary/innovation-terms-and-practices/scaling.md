# Scaling

Scaling is the process by which startups grow their operations, market reach, and revenue sustainably. It involves expanding business models that have demonstrated product-market fit, focusing on increasing capacity to serve more customers, entering new markets, and achieving long-term commercial success.
Did You Know
Startups that scale successfully leverage resources such as capital, talent, technology, and partnerships to optimize their growth. This phase requires strategic planning, robust operational structures, and often access to larger markets and investment.
Bonus Fact
A thriving innovation ecosystem links startups to global markets and capital, enhancing their ability to scale effectively.

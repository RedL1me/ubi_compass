# Product-Market Fit

Product-market fit is achieved when a product effectively meets the needs of a clearly defined target market, creating strong demand and customer satisfaction. It is the foundational milestone for startups that signals readiness for scaling, sustained growth, and competitive differentiation.
### Did You Know
Achieving product-market fit involves deep customer understanding, continuous feedback, and iterative product development. Startups analyze user behaviors, satisfaction levels, and market trends to refine value propositions and features.
### Bonus Fact
Incubators support this journey by facilitating market research, customer engagement, and structured experimentation to validate product assumptions.

# Stealth Mode

Stealth Mode refers to a temporary state in which a startup or business operates under strict secrecy to protect its innovative ideas, products, and strategic plans from competitors and market scrutiny. During this phase, limited information is shared publicly to safeguard intellectual property and maintain a competitive advantage until the company is ready for a public launch.
## Did You Know
Startups and established companies alike may adopt stealth mode to conduct product development, testing, and refinement away from public and media attention. This often involves non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) for employees and partners, codenames for projects, and restricted communications.
## Bonus Fact
Operating in stealth mode allows startups to refine their products without competitive pressure, reducing the risk of idea theft and market imitation. However, it may pose challenges in attracting investors, recruiting talent, and gathering early customer feedback.

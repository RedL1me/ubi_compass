# Iteration

Iteration is the cyclical process of developing, testing, and refining products, services, or strategies based on feedback and validation. Integral to startup methodology, it enables continuous enhancement through repeated adjustments, reducing risks and aligning offerings closely with market needs.
## Did You Know
The iterative process involves planning objectives, designing solutions, implementing changes, testing results, and reviewing feedback. Startups use iteration to deliver incremental improvements rapidly, incorporating user insights and performance data at each cycle.
## Bonus Fact
Incubators foster iteration by encouraging experimentation, facilitating user testing, and guiding adaptive learning.

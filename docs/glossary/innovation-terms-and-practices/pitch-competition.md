# Pitch Competition

A Pitch Competition is a structured event where entrepreneurs present their business ideas or startups to a panel of judges—often investors, industry experts, or business leaders—to secure funding, mentorship, recognition, or other resources that support business growth.
## Did You Know
Participants prepare concise, compelling presentations highlighting their value proposition, market potential, business model, and team strengths. Pitch competitions typically have eligibility criteria based on startup stage, sector, or geography. Judging is based on factors like innovation, scalability, team capability, and traction. Winners often receive cash prizes, mentorship, media exposure, or investment offers.

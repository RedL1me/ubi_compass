# Impact Investment

Impact investment refers to capital invested with the intentional goal of generating positive, measurable social or environmental impact alongside a financial return. This approach blends profit motives with purpose, seeking to address global challenges such as climate change, poverty, health, and education through responsible and accountable investing.
## Did You Know
Impact investments span a variety of asset classes and sectors, including renewable energy, affordable housing, microfinance, and sustainable agriculture. Investors actively measure and manage the social and environmental outcomes of their investments, using data-driven frameworks and standardized metrics.
## Bonus Fact
Innovation programs and incubators foster startups aligned with impact goals, helping navigate impact measurement and connect with impact-focused capital.

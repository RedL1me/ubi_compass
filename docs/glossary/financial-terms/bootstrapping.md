# Bootstrapping

Starting and growing a business using only personal funds or business-generated revenue, without external investment.
## Did you know...
Startups that bootstrap typically begin with founders’ personal savings and gradually reinvest revenue generated from early sales. They operate lean, focusing on cost-effective resources and often employing creative financing methods like presales or crowdfunding. Bootstrapped businesses tend to make cautious hires, prioritize customer-driven product development, and expand strategically to maintain financial stability and agility.
## Bootstrapping Action Steps for Incubators
- Encourage founders to leverage personal funds and early revenue
- Promote lean operations
- Support revenue generation early
- Provide training on creative financing
- Foster resource sharing
- Offer mentorship on financial discipline
- Build networks for customer and partner introductions

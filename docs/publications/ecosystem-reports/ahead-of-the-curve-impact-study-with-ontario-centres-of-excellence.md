# Ahead of the Curve - Impact Study with Ontario Centres of Excellence

## About
The IMPACT Study – Ahead of the Curve explores how Ontario’s Campus-Linked Accelerators (CLAs) and On-Campus Entrepreneurship Activities (OCEAs) have transformed the province’s academic landscape into a dynamic hub for youth entrepreneurship. Conducted by UBI Global in collaboration with Ontario Centres of Excellence (OCE) , the study assesses the impact of 59 accelerators and incubators across 42 academic institutions , revealing how they foster innovation, create jobs, and build stronger communities.
Evidence-based results. Real economic value.
With over 2,200 start-ups supported , 8,000 jobs created , and $85 million added to Ontario’s GDP in just two years, the study highlights the measurable results of government-backed entrepreneurship initiatives. It also captures the broader ripple effects — from enhanced regional ecosystems to record-high engagement of students, alumni, and young founders who are shaping Canada’s next generation of innovators.
Inspiring a global model for academic innovation.
Recognized internationally as a leading example of campus entrepreneurship, Ontario’s CLA and OCEA programs showcase how public investment and academic collaboration can catalyze sustainable, innovation-driven growth. The Ahead of the Curve study not only celebrates their success but also provides a roadmap for policymakers and educators worldwide aiming to strengthen the bridge between academia and entrepreneurship. 🌍✨

# KPIs

- Jobs Created & Sustained Number of jobs created and sustained by client startups and recent alumni
- Sales Revenue Total sales revenue generated by client startups and alumni
- Graduates Volume of startups completing the program
- Self-Generated Revenue Measurement of financial stability and independence
- Client Startups Accepted Number of client startups accepted into the program
- Graduate Retention Quantifies the number of alumni startups still active
- Services Offered Assessment of services offered to startups
- Coaching and Mentoring Hours Number of coaching and mentoring hours provided
- Total Investment Attracted Amount of investment attracted
- Average Investment Attracted Average amount of investment attracted per startup
- Seed Funding Attraction Amount of seed funding raised by client startups
- Partners Number of partners supporting the organization
- Events Number of events organized by the organization
- Alumni Engagement Number of alumni supporting the organization and its startups
- In-state Applications Number of in-state applications received
- Out-of-State Applications Number of out-of-state applications received
- Sponsorship Attraction Amount of financial sponsorship attracted
- 1-Year Survival Rate Percentage of alumni startups that survived for one year
- 5-Year Survival Rate Percentage of alumni startups that survived for five years
- High-Growth Enterprises Percentage of clients and/or alumni startups that were high growth
- Qualified Exits Number of enrollees to achieve IPOs and qualified acquisitions
